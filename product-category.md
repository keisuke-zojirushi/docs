ZAC SVC Site – Product Category Structure (Official)
Status: Approved / Official
Scope: ZAC SVC Site (Service & Technical Support)
Applies to: Product CPT, Sidebar Navigation, Archive Pages

1. Design Principles
This category structure is designed specifically for the ZAC SVC Site, not for marketing or e‑commerce.
Guiding principles:

Categories reflect service / repair responsibility, not sales segmentation
Structure aligns with existing ZAC models and Shopify public site taxonomy
Category count is optimized for sidebar navigation usability
Supports Phase 1 (browse & search) and Phase 2 (filtering & advanced UI)
